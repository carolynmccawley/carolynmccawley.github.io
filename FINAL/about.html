Welcome to Lire le Livre! Our policy is to read, read, and read! We encourage our readers to read a book a week. We recommend five books a week, but if none of the books there encourage you, we have a daily recommendation so everyone can find something they like. We want everyone to find the perfect book so we are constantly updating our website because there is always a book to be read and loved! Follow us on social media and give us your recommendations!

Lire Le Livre was founded in 2018 by Carolyn McCawley. She was inspired to create the website after reading Robinson Crusoe. She had never hated a book more and she wanted the world to have easy access to find a book they can enjoy.
