Student Calendar App

React Native App

The Student Calendar App was created so students can keep track of their daily schedule, exams, assignments, and other tasks on their to do list. They app uses Flux and Async Storage to keep track of and store data throughout the app.

The calendar app stores your completed todos and assignments in archives.
