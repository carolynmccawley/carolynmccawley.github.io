Build-a-Universe

Build-a-Universe is a game created using p5.js and is published on OpenProcessing. The user creates and controls their universe; they can control how many and which planets they want to create and the size of the planets.

After the user has created their universe, they are then guided to a quiz where they have to make decisions on how their planet is going to function. Based on their inputs, the fate of their universe will be calculated and displayed to the user.
